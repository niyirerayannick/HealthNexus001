Learn About Us

Welcome to YouthEmpower Health Nexus

The "YouthEmpower Health Nexus" is an innovative web application dedicated to enhancing reproductive health education for teenagers and young adults. Our mission is to bridge the gap in reproductive health knowledge, empowering young people to make informed decisions that lead to healthier lives.

Our Team

We are a group of IT graduates passionate about making a positive impact on the Rwandan community. By developing this platform, we aim to contribute to public health while improving our web development skills. This project also provides us with employment opportunities, allowing us to manage and maintain the application.

Institutional Support

This initiative aligns with the goals of the University of Rwanda - College of Science and Technology (UR_CST) by enhancing public health outcomes and promoting educational engagement. Our project supports comprehensive health literacy and fosters institutional partnerships, reflecting UR_CST's commitment to community health and education advancement.

Public Benefit

By providing accessible and engaging reproductive health education, we aim to reduce the rates of STIs, unintended pregnancies, and associated health risks. Our platform empowers young individuals with the knowledge and tools they need for a healthier future, benefiting the wider community and public health advocates.
